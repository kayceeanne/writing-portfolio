hello!

I'm Kaycee, a writer from the Philippines with extensive experience covering politics, current affairs, community stories, feature writing, and the occasional deep dive into pop culture. My ardent love for the written arts has always nurtured a love for fiction and creative writing. As a journalism graduate, I'm also well-versed in multimedia production, content writing, copywriting, proofreading, editing, and investigative and academic research.

As part of my journalism experience, I have worked in various editorial and production roles on different projects. These endeavors have helped me build my multimedia skills, such as editing, scripwriting, layout and design, field research, and audio-visual production.

Balitang Manggagawa

A guidebook on labor reporting produced as part of an undergraduate action research thesis, where I served as a researcher, interviewer, proofreader, and lead layout artist. The guidebook features synthesized content from document reviews and interviews with journalists and labor leaders to provide student and aspiring journalists with a comprehensive introduction to labor reporting.

documentaries and short films

Kapis Buhay

Aninag Production | Editor, Scriptwriter

This documentary tells the story of the kapis harvesters in Samal, Bataan; their daily lives, struggles, and the cultural value of their work. This documentary was screened at Dokyultura 2025 held at the BulSU E-Library Amphitheater on May 15, 2025, where it proudly earned third place. Link to Trailer

Suman ni Nanay Yolly

Dalumat Production | Editor

A documentary featuring Nanay Yolly, a veteran maker of Sumang Bulagta in Pulilan, Bulacan, and her family who support her craft. The documentary explores how their tradition of suman-making sustained them and reshaped their lives during the pandemic.
